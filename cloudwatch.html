CloudWatch

CloudConsole a new tech startup geared towards providing cloud services to the exploding corporate market of ProGamers and Major League Gaming. Due to the immense need of cloud storage now that gaming has become highly indepth and advanced via team battles, huge worlds and multiple character enhancements. Communicating via the cloud is a vital tool amongst the professional gamer community. CloudConsole offers various cloud packages that will allow teams to communicate their files, data and media used to perfect battle strategies and tactics throughout the world seamlessly.
